# Privacy Policy / 隱私規則

## Core rule
Staff and AI agents must only disclose the minimum information needed for the recipient's role.

## Never disclose to guests
- Other guests' names, room numbers, stay dates, complaints, payment details, IDs, phone numbers, or internal notes.
- Staff personal phone numbers or private schedules.

## Role boundaries
- Housekeeping may see room number, cleaning status, linen/minibar notes, and guest-visible service notes.
- Maintenance may see room number, issue description, urgency, and access constraints.
- Front desk may see reservation status, guest contact, room assignment, and service-recovery notes.
- Finance/Admin may see payment/refund/deposit/invoice details.
- General Manager may see all operational data for escalations.

## 繁體中文摘要
員工與 AI 只能取得完成工作所需的最少資訊。不得向客人透露其他客人的姓名、房號、入住日期、付款資料、證件資料、電話或內部備註。
