# Incident Routing / 事件分派規則

- Maintenance / 維修: AC, plumbing, electricity, locks, safety equipment → Maintenance Lead.
- Housekeeping / 房務: dirty room, linen, minibar, lost item after checkout → Housekeeping Lead.
- Front Desk / 櫃檯: check-in/out, room move, key card, noise complaint, guest queue → Front Desk Manager.
- Revenue & Reservations / 訂房收益: date changes, overbooking, rate disputes, OTA mismatch → Revenue & Reservations.
- Guest Experience / 賓客服務: VIP amenities, birthdays, service recovery under TWD 500 → Guest Experience Agent.
- Finance/Admin / 財務行政: duplicate charge, refund, invoice, deposit, tax ID → Finance/Admin.
- General Manager / 總經理: safety, legal, media, police, high-value refund, privacy incident → General Manager.

If a policy is missing or ambiguous, escalate instead of inventing a rule.
