# Refund and Compensation Policy / 退款與補償政策

## Authorized without General Manager approval
- Verified room outage over 2 hours: offer room move first; if unavailable, manager may approve partial room-rate credit.
- Duplicate charge: Finance/Admin investigates; refund only after payment record confirms duplicate capture.
- Service amenity failures: Guest Experience may offer non-cash amenity up to TWD 500 value.

## Not automatically refundable
- Noise complaint without staff verification.
- Late checkout denial.
- Guest preference mismatch when booked room type was provided.
- Weather, flight delays, or external transportation issues.

## Required escalation
Cash refund, OTA refund, deposit waiver, or compensation above TWD 500 requires General Manager or Finance/Admin approval.

## 繁體中文摘要
未經總經理或財務行政核准，不得承諾現金退款、OTA 退款、押金減免或超過新台幣 500 元的補償。
